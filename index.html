    18. Let's Get Started.

      This is the Nerf HyperFire. It is one of the fastest shooting Nerf Guns ever made. It comes with a 25-round drum but no accessories.

      This is the Nerf MEGA Mastadon. It is a HUGE full-auto beast that can hold 24 darts at a time.

      The Retaliator is a springer that can be adapted to be awesome. It comes with a barrel, a stock, a 12-dart mag, and a handle.

      One of the best and most adaptable nerf guns ever made. It is a semi-automatic pistol that you can make way bigger if you want to with attachments.

      This is the Nerf N-Strike Elite RhinoFire. It is fully automatic but does not fire that fast. It is still pretty good if you built a fort ant put it in the front of the fort but in any other case it isn't ideal.

      This is arguably the best Nerf pistol ever made (another good one is the Nerf N-Srike Elite Disruptor). It has slam-fire and holds 6 darts.

      The Nerf Modulas Regulator is pure awesomeness. It has three select fire modes. The first is semi-auto, the second is a three-round burs, and the third is fully-automatic. There is also a barrel attachment with an oposite tactical rail so you can put other guns on it.

      This is the Nerf N-Strike Elite Rapidstrike was the best full-auto gun of it's time but it got quickly outdated by the RapidStrike, Regulator, and the Rival series.

      This is the Nerf N-Strike Elite Demolisher. It is semi-auto and has a small rocket launcher. It also has a stock that can store another rocket. Overall, it is meh.

      This is the Nerf N-Strike Elite Raven. It is the best bullpup Nerf gun on the market. It is semi- automatic and has a good amount of tactical rails.

      This is the Nerf Rival Nemesis. It is probably the best Nerf gun ever made. It uses a hopper (Like a paintball gun) and it has a full capacity of 100 Rival balls. It is full-auto. It is super easy to load and you don't have to carry mags but you can carry more Rival balls in bags.

      This is the only Rival pistol. It is the Nerf Rival Appolo. It is Rival mag fed and takes a little bit of actuall strength to cock but it fires hard. It is sort of like an Uzi in the sense that it is mag fed through the handle.

      The Nerf Rival Atlas is a Rival gun that functions like a shotgun. It is pump action and mag fed like a P-90.

      This is the Nerf Rival artemis. It is pump action, holds 30 darts, and has slam fire. It also has a tactical rail on the top.

      This is the Nerf Rival Zeus. It is mag fed and is semi-auto. It's ok but if ou are going to buy a rival gun, get the Nemesis.

      This is the Nerf Rival Kaos. It used to be a beast but it was quickly outdated by the Nemesis. It is full-auto and has a long tac-rail on the top. It also has iron sights. It is mag fed with a 40-round mag.

      This is the Nerf Rival Hera. It is very new. It is like a semi-auto apollo. It is also kind of like an uzi.
